Return Policy

At Blaze City, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not happy with your order, we offer easy returns and exchanges.

Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or exchange, your item must:

  • Be in the original condition
  • Be unused and undamaged
  • Have all original packaging and tags
  • Be returned within 30 days of delivery

How to Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Email us at info@blazecity.com with your order number and reason for return
  2. We will send you a return shipping label
  3. Package the item securely and attach the return label
  4. Drop off the package at your nearest shipping location

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • We will process your refund within 3-5 business days
  • Refunds will be issued to your original payment method
  • You will receive an email notification when the refund is processed

Exchanges

If you would like to exchange your item for a different size, color, or product:

  • Follow the return process above
  • Include a note with your order number and the item you would like to exchange for
  • We will ship the new item once we receive your return

Non-Returnable Items

Some items are non-returnable for safety and hygiene reasons:

  • Opened or used lighters
  • Personal care items
  • Custom or personalized items

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund
