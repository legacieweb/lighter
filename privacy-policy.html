Privacy Policy

At Blaze city, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing address
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through Stripe)
  • Account Information: Username, password, and purchase history
  • Usage Information: IP address, browser type, device information, and website activity

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Process and ship your orders
  • Send you order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to your customer service inquiries
  • Personalize your shopping experience
  • Send you marketing emails (if you opt in)
  • Improve our website and services
  • Prevent fraud and protect our business

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We share information with trusted service providers to process payments, ship orders, and provide customer support
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: Information may be transferred in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. This includes:

  • Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption for all sensitive data
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls to our systems

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)

Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your shopping experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track shopping cart contents
  • Analyze website traffic
  • Personalize ads

You can control cookie settings through your browser.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.
